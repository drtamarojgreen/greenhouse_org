# Scene 5: The Glasshouse Grumble (Expanded)

**Herbaceous**: (Vibrant green, but looking a bit wilted)
**Arbor**: (Stalwart mahogany tree humanoid)

---

### Phase 1: The Sprouting Anxiety (0 - 900 Frames)

**Herbaceous**: "Arbor, do you ever feel like your sap is just... running thin? No matter how much sunlight I get, my leaves still feel heavy."
*(Herbaceous droops their head slightly and shivers. Eyes darting nervously. (shiver, droop, worry). HERBACEOUS_V5)*

**Arbor**: "It happens to the best of us, little sprout. The seasons change, and sometimes the internal pressure drops. You’re not wilting; you’re just adjusting."
*(Arbor nods slowly and reaches out with an arm towards Herbaceous. (nod, reach_out, relief). ARBOR_V5)*

**Herbaceous**: "But the others... they look so vibrant. They’re branching out so quickly, and I’m just trying to keep my stem straight."
*(Herbaceous looks down, arms held close to the torso. (look_down, worry). HERBACEOUS_V5)*

---

### Phase 2: Rooted Wisdom (900 - 1800 Frames)

**Arbor**: "Comparison is the parasite that kills the bloom. Remember, your roots are deeper than you think. We’re all connected to the same mycelium down here. You’re never growing alone."
*(Arbor stands tall, arms raised high in a welcoming gesture. (stretch, power_pose). ARBOR_V5)*

**Herbaceous**: "Connected... I forget that sometimes. When the soil gets dry, it feels like I’m in a pot by myself."
*(Herbaceous looks up at Arbor, eyes widening slightly. (look_up, relief). HERBACEOUS_V5)*

**Arbor**: "Then reach out! Send a signal through the fungi. We’re here to share the nutrients—whether it’s water or just a bit of hope."
*(Arbor smiles and does a gentle torso swayed. (smile, sway). ARBOR_V5)*

---

### Phase 3: The Resilient Bloom (1800 - 3600 Frames)

**Herbaceous**: "I think I feel a bit more... turgid. Like the water is finally reaching my top-most leaves."
*(Herbaceous stretches upwards, arms reaching for the glass ceiling. (stretch, deep_breath, relief). HERBACEOUS_V5)*

**Arbor**: "There it is. That's the pulse. You just needed to remember that even the oldest oak was once a sprout who felt a bit unstable."
*(Arbor beams with pride, arms waving slowly in the air. (smile, celebrate). ARBOR_V5)*

**Herbaceous**: "Thanks, Arbor. I might just make it to the next harvest after all."
*(Herbaceous does a small happy wiggle. (wiggle, joyful). HERBACEOUS_V5)*

---

### New Animation Definitions
- **shiver**: High-frequency, low-amplitude vibration of the torso and limbs.
- **droop**: Gradual lowering of the head and upper torso to signify low energy/mood.
- **stretch**: An expansive movement raising the head and extending the arms to their full range.
- **wiggle**: A rhythmic, side-to-side torso and hip sway.
- **reach_out**: One arm extends towards the other character in a supportive gesture.

### New Facial Expressions
- **worry**: Eyebrows pulled together and up, lip corners slightly lowered.
- **relief**: Eyebrows returning to neutral, eyes widening, and a soft lip relaxation.
- **joyful**: High eyebrows, wide smile, and pupil dilation.
- **exhaustion**: Heavy eyelids (half-closed), lower head pitch, and slow pupil darting.
