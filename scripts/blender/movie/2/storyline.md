# The Verdant Pulse: Movie Treatment (v2.0)

## Narrative Concept
A hero's journey set within a high-tech "Seed Vault." The Greenhouse Guardian must save the last digital seedling from a chaotic entropy known as "The Blight."

## Characters
*   **Liana (The Guardian)**: A tall, graceful character made of glowing vines and translucent leaves. She moves with a rhythmic, pulsing light.
*   **The Seedling (The Protagonist)**: A tiny, vibrant sprout that sits at the center of the vault. Its "heartbeat" drives the movie's soundtrack.
*   **The Blight (The Antagonist)**: A slow-moving, oily shadow that absorbs light and color, threatening to consume the seedling.

## Act-by-Act Plot
*   **Act I: The Intrusion (0 - 2500)**: The Seed Vault is a clean, metallic space. The camera zooms in on the Seedling. We see the "Blight" creeping into the irrigation pipes. Liana awakens when the Seedling's pulse weakens.
*   **Act II: The Infrastructure Walk (2501 - 5500)**: Liana travels through the skeletal structural beams of the vault. This is a high-contrast sequence using "Solar Core" lighting. She must clear a blockage in the "Light Grate."
*   **Act III: The Bloom (5501 - 8500)**: Liana pulls a lever, the Grate opens, and the vault is flooded with 5.0 intensity Sunlight. The Blight is vaporized. In the finale, the metallic vault transforms—vines grow over the beams, and the floor turns to rich soil—creating the first "Greenhouse."

## Technical Guardrails (The Promise)
1.  **Visually Pleasing**: High-contrast transition from cold "Blue/Grey" (Act I) to vibrant "Green/Gold" (Act III).
2.  **Clear Corridors**: The environment is made of structural pipes and beams. The camera tracks Liana along these lines, ensuring **zero** obstructions from trees or rocks.
3.  **No Z-Shifting**: All "hidden" objects stay at their coordinates with `hide_render` enabled, preventing the "underground" bug.
4.  **Cycles-Optimized**: Materials use Subsurface Scattering (SSS) for leaves to create a premium, translucent look.
